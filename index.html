Esben Klemann

Esben Klemann is a Danish artist, whose work consists mainly in the forms of sculptures, installations and illustrations.

He is born in 1972 and studied at Det Kongelige Danske Kunstakademi and Det Jyske Kunstakademi from the years 1996-2000. His work has been displayed in various locations throughout Denmark, Bornholm and Sweden. He has as ongoing exhibition in Paris, at the Maria Lund Gallery.

Esben is an interdisciplinary artist, who approaches all media with a playful attitude, constantly attempting to subjugate all materials, following the rule of “systematic uncertainty”. His constructions are contemporary, poetic and amusing follies, snapshots of an omnipotent freedom. Click here if you want to see further images of is work.
